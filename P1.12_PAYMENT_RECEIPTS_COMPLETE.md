## What Was Delivered

### Professional Payment Receipts for Completed Payments
Tenants and landlords can now view beautifully formatted payment receipts for all completed payments, with the ability to share them via text, email, or other apps.

**Features**:
- **Professional Receipt Design**: Clean, official-looking receipt layout
- **Complete Payment Details**: Amount, method, period, status, transaction ID, notes
- **Property Information**: Property name, unit, tenant name (when available)
- **Native Sharing**: Share receipt text via iOS/Android share sheet
- **Easy Access**: Tap completed payments to view receipt
- **Visual Hierarchy**: Clear sections with professional formatting
- **Receipt Numbers**: Short, memorable receipt IDs (first 8 characters of payment ID)

---

## How It Works

### User Flow

**Tenant Workflow**:
```
1. Tenant opens PaymentHistoryScreen
2. Sees completed payment: "$2,500 - February 2026 - ✓ Completed"
3. Notices hint: "📄 Tap for receipt"
4. Taps payment card
5. PaymentReceiptScreen opens with:
   - Receipt header: "PAYMENT RECEIPT"
   - Receipt #: ABC12345
   - Property: Sunset Apartments
   - Unit: 2B
   - Amount: $2,500
   - Payment period: February 2026
   - Method: Credit Card
   - Status: COMPLETED
6. Tenant taps "📤 Share Receipt"
7. Native share sheet opens
8. Shares via:
   - Email to accountant
   - Text to roommate
   - Save to Files app
   - Print (iOS AirPrint)
```

**Landlord Workflow** (Future enhancement):
```
1. Landlord views PropertyPaymentsScreen
2. Sees completed payment from tenant
3. Taps payment card
4. Views receipt with all details
5. Can share receipt for records
```

  await Share.share({
    message: receiptText,
    title: 'Payment Receipt',
  });

## Known Limitations (MVP Scope)

### Current Limitations
1. **Text-Only Receipts** - No PDF generation (plain text sharing)
2. **No Automatic Email** - Must manually share
3. **No Receipt Storage** - Not saved separately (just view from payment)
4. **No Batch Export** - Cannot export all receipts at once
5. **No Custom Branding** - Fixed "Naya Property Management" branding
6. **No Barcodes/QR** - No machine-readable identification

**All limitations are intentional for MVP and can be enhanced later**
